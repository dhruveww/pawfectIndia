About

Who we are

Pawfect India is an animal NGO dedicated to improving the lives of animals in India. We are a team of passionate animal lovers who believe in the importance of animal welfare and protection.At Pawfect India, we are driven by our love for animals and our desire to create a better world for them.

  • Rescue and Rehabilitation
  • Awareness and Education
  • Advocating for stronger animal welfare laws and policies.

Pawfect India is an animal NGO that functions on various levels to improve the lives of animals in India. The organization conducts workshops and campaigns to educate people about the importance of treating animals with kindness and compassion. The organization's ultimate goal is to create a world where animals are treated with respect and compassion, and where their welfare is given the importance it deserves.

Protect and Promote Welfare of Animals

Pawfect India's primary goal is to protect and promote the welfare of animals. This can include rescuing animals, providing medical care, and educating the public on animal rights and welfare issues.

  • Vaccinating Animals
  • Protecting against human agony

We also implement programs such as spay and neuter programs to control animal populations, educate the public about responsible pet ownership, and collaborate with other organizations to achieve their goals. The overall goal of animal NGOs is to ensure that animals are treated with kindness and respect and are able to live a life free from abuse and neglect.

Rescue and Rehabilitation of Animals

Pawfect India provide rescue and rehabilitation services for animals in need, including those that have been abused, abandoned, or neglected. Our organizations may also provide temporary shelter and foster care for animals until they can be adopted into permanent homes.

The rescue and rehabilitation process involves providing medical care, nutrition, and shelter to the animals in need, and addressing any behavioral or emotional issues.

  • We respond to emergencies such as natural disasters, animal hoarding cases, and other situations that require immediate attention to rescue animals in distress.
  • We provide shelter to rescued animals, ensuring they have access to clean water, food, and a comfortable living space.
  • We also provide rehabilitation services to injured or orphaned wildlife, preparing them for release back into their natural habitat.

Services

What we do offer

Adoption and foster care

Pawfect India provide adoption and foster care services for homeless pets, ensuring that animals find loving homes where they can thrive.

Spay and neuter programs

Pawfect India promote spaying and neutering to help control the pet population and prevent animal homelessness.

Education and outreach:

Pawfect India offer educational programs and resources to promote responsible pet ownership and raise awareness about animal welfare issues

Advocacy and lobbying

Pawfect India advocate for animal welfare policies and work with lawmakers to enact change that benefits animals.

Animal-assisted therapy

Pawfect India offer animal-assisted therapy programs, where trained animals are used to improve the physical and emotional well-being of people in need.

Disaster response

Pawfect India provide disaster response services to assist animals affected by natural disasters, such as hurricanes, floods, and wildfires.

Volunteer for Pawfect Inida

DO YOU LOVE TAKING CARE OF DOGS?



There are many different ways to volunteer at Pawfect India.

We have so much going on at Pawfect India because taking care of dogs is more than a full-time job. It requires a heart and it requires a soul. If you have both, come volunteer with us. We have listed out a few different ways in which you can.


1.pawfect india sanctuary

Assist our staff to look after our dogs. Duties include cleaning after and caring for dogs, especially if they are undergoing medical care. This is a hands-on job that requires a high degree of compassion and skill. You must have a moderate-to-high level of experience dealing with dogs that are sick and/or aggressive. This job requires you to stay at the pawfect Sanctuary for the duration of your internship.


2.pawfect india publishing

We are looking for interns and volunteers who can make a difference with in-depth stories, photo-essays, films, and research that depict the realities of strays.This is an opportunity to change the way people perceive stray dogs.You are not required to stay at the pawfect Sanctuary for this role.


3.Digital Outreach, Fundraising & Events

At pawfect india, we pride ourselves in the marketing skills of our team. We hire the best and brightest, and if you join, you will get the work with them!Our team is full of fantastic branding, marketing, and fundraising experts.You are not required to stay at the pawfect Sanctuary for this role.
